About Me

I am Gilbert Ngendahimana and I was born and raised in Rwanda where I spent all my childhood. Currently I live in Ottawa, and I am a student at Algonquin College in the IMD program. I like to develop web applications and the idea came when I was in my 10th grade. I like to go out and enjoy life with my family and friends.

Growing up, I have always wanted to travel abroad and change my environment and explore other countries. Coming to study in Canada was a best option for me as I am learning new leaving style and meeting with new people from different countries. Even though the weather is not friendly during winter, I can still adapt easily.
